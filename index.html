Server Placement and Task Allocation for Load Balancing in Edge-Computing Networks

While working as a Research Assistant at the Mobile Computing and Networking Lab, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, I co-authored a research paper on improving computing services by offloading tasks to edge servers. This research aimed to reduce delays seen with cloud computing by smartly placing edge servers and evenly spreading out the workload. We created a new method using simulated annealing to find the best places for servers and used Lagrangian duality with the sub-gradient method for task allocation. Our tests showed that our method works better than older approaches, making user experiences better in mobile computing settings.
